Lewis Hamilton

Sir Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton ( born 7 January 1985 ) is a British racing driver.
Seven-time Formula One World Champion in 2008 , 2014 , 2015 , 2017 , 2018 , 2019 and 2020.
Hamilton is one of the greatest drivers of all time, and one of the most successful sportsmen in history.
